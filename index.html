About

I work where financial structure meets physical reality.

I focus on cross-border investments in mining and infrastructure, primarily in emerging markets. My work sits between financial structuring, project development, and capital deployment.

Over time, I have been involved in large-scale resource and infrastructure projects where complexity is shaped not just by numbers, but by sovereign risk, logistics, energy constraints, regulation, and the actual mechanics of execution.

I am especially interested in a simple question: what makes a real asset genuinely investable? Not in theory, but in practice.
