Field Projects

West Africa · Guinea

Iron Ore Project

Landmark African iron ore — integrated mine, rail, and port — with Guinea field work and lender-style bankability framing.

Worked on a landmark African iron ore project with potential to reshape global seaborne supply through a large-scale mine, rail, and port buildout. Combined on-site due diligence in Guinea with analysis of country risk, transport costs, infrastructure capex, complex structuring, and lender-style bankability considerations to support internal investment assessment.

Investment assessment · Integrated logistics

East Africa · Eritrea

Gold Mining Project

Early-stage Eritrea gold outside the main belts — investment view anchored on a strong cost position versus a >$2,600/oz market.

Evaluated an early-stage gold project in Eritrea, a promising smaller-scale resource outside the better-known gold belts of South Africa and West Africa. Supported a positive investment view despite gold already trading above US$2,600/oz, based on the project's attractive cost position with AISC of around US$1,100/oz.

Early-stage resource · Cost-curve thesis

Central Africa · DRC

Hard-Rock Lithium Project

Large-scale open-pit LCT pegmatite in DRC — internal read on merits, risks, and structuring for a globally significant undeveloped deposit.

Analyzed a DRC lithium project with large-scale open-pit potential, widely regarded as one of the world's largest high-grade undeveloped LCT pegmatite deposits. Supported internal assessment of project merits, key risks, and structuring considerations.

Hard-rock lithium · Technical & commercial DD

Southern Africa · Zambia

Grid-Connected Solar

Small grid-connected solar in Zambia — built a working view on African IPP bankability, then packaged it into a reusable project-finance toolkit.

Worked on a small grid-connected solar project in Zambia and developed a deep working view on the bankability of African IPP transactions, including tariff design, payment security, PPA risk allocation, and financing feasibility. Built a lightweight project finance tool and a reusable analytical framework to assess similar solar opportunities across African markets.
