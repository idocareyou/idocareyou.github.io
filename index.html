East Africa · Eritrea

Gold Mining Project

Early-stage Eritrea gold outside the main belts — investment view anchored on a strong cost position versus a >$2,600/oz market.

Evaluated an early-stage gold project in Eritrea, a promising smaller-scale resource outside the better-known gold belts of South Africa and West Africa. Supported a positive investment view despite gold already trading above US$2,600/oz, based on the project's attractive cost position with AISC of around US$1,100/oz.
